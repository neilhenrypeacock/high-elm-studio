a note on how we work

Every business is different. Every AI application is different. And the way we work — whether that's with a sole trader streamlining one process or an established company transforming how an entire team operates — can be different too.

What you see here is a starting framework, not a fixed formula. The conversation, the presentation, Build and Grow are the shape of how we typically work. But the scope, the sequence, the pace, and the scale of any engagement is always shaped around what actually makes sense for your business.

If something doesn't fit the framework, we'll say so. And we'll find the approach that does.
