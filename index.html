about

High Elm Studio was built on a simple belief: that the businesses who communicate clearly, automate intelligently, and act decisively are the ones who pull ahead. AI is just the latest — and most powerful — tool to make that possible.

The road
that led here.

It begins on a farm in Norfolk. Then seven years in London — learning the pace, the pressure, the scale of big brand work. When the world shut down, a return to green fields and a slower sky.

Cornwall came calling. A drive-in cinema launched on the cliffs of Watergate Bay.

Then Bristol — the inaugural Blue Earth Summit. A transfer to Sri Lanka, leading a global surf and ski agency. Two years in Portugal: dream homes in Lisbon, chalets in the Alps, a thought leadership festival in Türkiye.

The final destination was Indonesia — building a marina entertainment space from the ground up, and launching a luxury seaplane brand for UHNW travellers.

Twelve years. Six countries. One consistent thread: understanding what makes serious businesses work, and helping them communicate and operate better.

the thread

Story, automation,
and the moment they met.

Long before AI entered the picture, the work was always the same: understand a business deeply, then build the clearest possible bridge between it and the people it serves.

That meant story automations — the systems that turn what a brand knows and feels into consistent, purposeful communication at scale. Email sequences that read like a person wrote them. Guest journeys that feel considered at every touchpoint. Brand voices that hold across a hundred different moments.

And then AI arrived. Not as a replacement for any of that — but as the engine that makes it possible to do it faster, smarter, and at a scale that simply wasn't accessible before.

The businesses winning right now are the ones who understand that AI is a communication tool as much as an operational one. The clarity of your brief to an AI system directly determines the quality of what comes out. Precision in, precision out.

01

story automations

Your brand's voice, values, and experience — mapped into intelligent systems that communicate consistently without constant manual effort. Guest welcome journeys. Client onboarding flows. Follow-up sequences that sound like you.

the foundation of every high-performing AI system
02

communication design

How a message is structured is as important as what it says. Designing the architecture of communication — the sequence, the tone, the trigger, the timing — is what separates AI that builds trust from AI that erodes it.

where brand thinking meets systems thinking
03

bridging the gap

Most businesses don't struggle with AI because the technology is too complex. They struggle because the brief is too vague. We help you translate what you know about your business into the clear, structured language that makes AI produce results that actually work.
