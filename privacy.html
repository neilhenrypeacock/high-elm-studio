privacy policy.

last updated: march 2026

who we are

High Elm Studio is the trading name of High Elm Productions Ltd (Companies House number: 15336186), an AI implementation agency. We help established businesses find, build and embed AI that earns its place.

We are based in the United Kingdom. You can contact us at hello@highelm.studio.

what information we collect

When you use our website or chat with our AI assistant, we may collect:

  • Messages you send through the chat interface — including any details you share about your business, challenges, or goals
  • Your name and email address if you choose to share them
  • Basic usage data — which pages you visit, how long you spend on them, and what device or browser you use
  • Cookie data — see our Cookie Policy

We do not collect sensitive personal data (such as financial information, health data, or data relating to children).

why we collect it

We use this information to:

  • Understand your business situation and respond with a personalised video or message
  • Improve how the website and chat tool work
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to anyone.

how the chat works

Our chat interface is powered by AI. Specifically, we use the Anthropic API (the technology behind Claude) to process and respond to your messages.

This means that messages you send may be processed by Anthropic's systems as part of generating a response. Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to that processing: anthropic.com/privacy.

Your conversation is also reviewed by Neil Summers (founder, High Elm Studio) so that he can follow up with a personalised response.

how long we keep your data

  • Chat conversations: retained for up to 12 months, then deleted
  • Contact information you share: retained for as long as we have an active relationship with you
  • Website analytics: retained for up to 26 months in aggregate form

You can ask us to delete your data at any time.

who we share your data with

We share data only with:

  • Anthropic — to power the chat interface (see above)
  • Netlify — our website host, which may log basic request data
  • Our analytics provider — in anonymised, aggregated form only

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccuracies
  • Delete your data (the right to be forgotten)
  • Object to how we use it
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe we have handled your data incorrectly: ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@highelm.studio and we will respond within 30 days.

cookies

We use a small number of cookies to make the website work and to understand how people use it. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the banner on our website.

For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

changes to this policy

If we make significant changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. We recommend checking back periodically.
